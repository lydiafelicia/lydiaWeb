Cheesesteak is a sandwich made from thinly sliced pieces of steak and melted cheese in a long hoagie roll. A popular regional fast food, it has its roots in Philadelphia.

We, the Philly Cheesesteak Truck, go around Philadelphia to serve the local with our hearty, made from fresh ingredients authentic Philadelphian cheesesteak.
