Cheesesteak is a sandwich made from thinly sliced pieces of steak and melted cheese in a long hoagie roll. A popular regional fast food, it has its roots in Philadelphia.

We, the Philly Cheesesteak Truck, go around the city of Philadelphia to serve the local with our hearty, made from fresh ingredients authentic Philadelphian cheesesteak.

So come by and get our delicious cheesesteak when you are in area! We promise our cheesesteak will be the best cheesesteak you will ever have!
